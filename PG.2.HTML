Noise of Directions: A 3D Visual Labyrinth

The project centers around a surreal, overcrowded signpost where languages (Arabic and English) intertwine and directives conflict. From signs demanding a halt to others urging movement, and from cues enticing stardom to those enforcing strict paths, the composition creates a deep sense of paradox. Foggy, atmospheric lighting and glowing neon elements were meticulously designed to build a dramatic environment that reflects psychological ambiguity. This project is a visual exploration of transforming abstract human emotions into tangible textures and an immersive 3D space.
